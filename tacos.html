Tacos

Tacos are made with ground beef and taco shells. Start by grounding the beef in a skillet, adding taco seasoning and water, bring to a boil and stir for 5 minutes. Then add to shells and include your favorite ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Shells
  • Taco Sauce

Steps

  1. Brown meat
  2. Add taco seasoning and water
  3. Boil and stir for 5 minutes
  4. Add favorite ingredients
